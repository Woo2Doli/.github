# Woo2Doli – Smart E‑Commerce & ERP Bridge Tool

**Woo2Doli** is a powerful, flexible integration solution designed to help online stores and ERP systems work together, synchronize orders, and achieve operational clarity.  
With **Woo2Doli**, you can transform chaotic manual data entry into automated workflows.  
**Woo2Doli** supports order sync, product updates, and customer alignment, making it an essential productivity companion for store owners, accountants, and operations teams.

## About Woo2Doli

**Woo2Doli** combines the simplicity of a plugin with the power of a full bridge. Unlike basic integration tools, **Woo2Doli** offers deep customization: field mapping, webhook control, and error handling.  
**Woo2Doli** works seamlessly with your existing WooCommerce store and Dolibarr ERP and provides real‑time updates across both systems.  
Whether you manage a small shop or a multi‑warehouse business, **Woo2Doli** adapts to your workflow, not the other way around.  
**Woo2Doli** respects your data – all information stays under your control with local processing and optional logging.  
With **Woo2Doli**, you gain clarity, reduce manual work, and process more orders every day.

## Key Features of Woo2Doli

- **Order Sync** – **Woo2Doli** automatically transfers new orders to Dolibarr.  
- **Product Updates** – **Woo2Doli** synchronizes stock levels and prices.  
- **Customer Alignment** – Create or update customers inside **Woo2Doli**.  
- **Invoice Generation** – **Woo2Doli** triggers invoices in Dolibarr after payment.  
- **Webhook Support** – Process real‑time events with **Woo2Doli**.  
- **Error Logging** – Track failed syncs inside **Woo2Doli** with detailed messages.  
- **Field Mapping** – Customize which fields **Woo2Doli** transfers.  
- **Batch Processing** – Sync hundreds of orders at once using **Woo2Doli**.  
- **Recurring Jobs** – Set hourly, daily, or custom intervals with **Woo2Doli**.  
- **Attachments & Notes** – Add sync notes inside **Woo2Doli** for auditing.  
- **Team Collaboration** – Share sync rules and logs via **Woo2Doli**.  
- **Export & Backup** – Export your **Woo2Doli** configuration to JSON.  
- **Cross‑Platform** – Use **Woo2Doli** on any server with PHP 7.4+.  
- **Keyboard Shortcuts** – Navigate **Woo2Doli** admin panel faster.  
- **Push Notifications** – Get alerts from **Woo2Doli** when sync fails.  
- **Templates** – Save mapping profiles inside **Woo2Doli** for reuse.  
- **Search & Replace** – Find any order or log instantly using **Woo2Doli** search.
